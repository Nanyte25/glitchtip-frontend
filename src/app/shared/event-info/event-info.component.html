Events

Events are the unit of measure for tracking GlitchTip usage. An organization's maximum number of events per month is determined by its subscription type. There are three kinds of events:

  • Errors: Each time your app sends an error report to GlitchTip
  • Uptime: Whenever GlitchTip checks the status of your app or your app sends a request to GlitchTip to report its own status
  • Performance: Each time your app sends a performance status report to GlitchTip
